Zahroof Mohamed - President & CTO
Zahroof has over 20 years of experience in top engineering and R&D positions and has successfully brought several products from concept to market. He is the inventor of the SF and the founder of ZVI. He was most recently Chief Engineer for Samsung Techwin, establishing their Center for Turbomachinery Design World-wide in Houston and overseeing engineering development in South Korea and USA. Prior to this he worked with Cameron Compression Systems, York International and Atlas Copco, leading the development of several successful industrial compressors. His expertise includes high speed rotating machinery design; oil and gas hydrodynamic bearings; roller, hydrostatic and magnetic bearings; high and low speed gas seals; gearbox; centrifugal impeller aero-mechanical design; centrifugal, screw (oil free, oil-injected and water injected), reciprocating, scroll and tooth compressors; turbochargers; compressor valves; refrigeration cycles and systems; and high speed electric motor (permanent magnet, induction and SRM) integration . He has several patents and inventions in these fields. Zahroof has an MS in Mechanical Engineering from Texas A&M University, a Diploma in International Management from IFL (Swedish Management Institute) and is a Six Sigma Green Belt.

Bindu has more than 25 years of experience working primarily as an educator. Dan Rogers - VP, Sales & Marketing
Dan brings 15 years of compression experience with a heavy focus on customer service and commercial operations. Before joining ZVI, Dan worked five years for GE Oil & Gas where he held the roles of Sr. Manager, Regional Sales for Gas Processing & LNG and Sr. Sales Leader, North America for Gemini Compressors. Prior to GE, Dan worked for 10 years at Hoerbiger Corporation where he held progressive roles in Engineering, Sales, Product Management, and Strategic Accounts Management. Dan began his Oil & Gas career with Schlumberger where he spent four years as a field service engineer in Texas and off-shore Gulf of Mexico.
Dan is a graduate of Auburn University where he received a Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering.
