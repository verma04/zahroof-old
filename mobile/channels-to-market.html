Working directly with customers to get the best results for your compressor plate valve needs.

Zahroof Valves Inc works closely with our customers to understand clearly their compressor plate valve requirements and then to optimize an engineered solution that will provide the best results in terms of performance and reliability. We provide all engineering support and address all questions regarding the use of these compressor valves in different applications. Zahroof Valves Inc currently works with a handful of non-exclusive partners outside the US, who can provide our customers with added value services.

The StraightFlo Valves are a new technology, quite different from conventional compressor plate valves in appearance and operation.
  • Experience with other valve types do not necessarily carry over to the StraightFlo valves.
  • TheStraightFlo valve does not require machining during servicing or anywhere near the level of service or repair that conventional compressor plate valves require.
  • It is important that the valves are sized correctly for an application.
All technical questions regarding application and limitations go directly to the engineering department of Zahroof Valves Inc. who are the only personnel qualified to answer these questions. ZVI will optimize the valves for the application, and provide all technical support to make sure that the customer understands the value proposition and limitations of the valve fully.

Direct Channels

Zahroof Valves, Inc. maintains direct channels with compressor manufacturers and companies with a large operating pool of compressors that make a centralized purchasing decision. Typically, any company that has the ability and know how to fill in the RFQ, is comfortable placing orders over e-mail, can install and remove their own valves, and can perform the basic replacement of modules required for the service of SF valves should work directly with ZVI. Compressor manufacturers can take advantage of the unique design of the SF valvess and work closely with ZVI's engineering team to customize their compressor designs to make differentiated products that offer better performance, better value and opens up new markets. Companies that depend on a centralized engineering decision based on rigorous engineering studies, tests, and evaluations can also benefit from working directly and closely with ZVI's engineering team to get optimum solutions for their compression valve needs that is not available with conventional valves.

If you would like to integrate our valve into your design, would like to improve your compressor's reliability/performance/cost of operation or have a valve related reciprocating compression problem that you want to solve, please contact us at support@zahroofvalves.com

Partners

Outside the US, Zahroof Valves, Inc. will gradually grow a network of sales representatives and service providers to channel our valves to a section of our customers. Contact ZVI directly for your needs and ZVI will direct you to the channel that is best suited to serve you. Customers may benefit from the local presence and added value services provided by our partners while still relying on ZVI's engineering expertise and products to solve their valve related problems. Typically, partners are the preferred channel when a customer prefers to use a service provider for added services that ZVI cannot provide or would like a local presence in addition to the services offered by ZVI.
