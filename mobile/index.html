Who is ZVI?

Zahroof Valves, Inc. (ZVI) is an innovative company, with its headquarters in Houston, Texas, that has developed and now markets the revolutionary StraightFlo valve for use in reciprocating gas compressors. The StraightFlo valve delivers a step change in valve performance, serviceability and reliability. As a result of these proven and measured improvements, ZVI is able to guarantee a 10% improvement in valve efficiency and a 100% increase in compressor MTBS (Mean Time Between Service, valve related) with an industry leading 10 year warranty on the stainless steel valve housing. For slow speed applications (below 600 rpm) with good quality gas, ZVI offers a 5 year limited warranty for the valve internals.

Advanced Engineering

Zahroof Valves, Inc. uses state of the art engineering tools to design its products and to provide support to its customers. Our valves are designed using 3D CAD, FEA, CFD and in-house proprietary programs that include state of the art compressor simulation and valve dynamics software using NIST based real gas routines.

Our staff of engineers will optimize each valve for your particular application so you can count on our guarantee for the best in performance, reliability, and a substantial reduction in operational cost. 

In addition to custom-engineering the products we
make, ZVI can provide engineering services for valve analysis, upgrades, and conversions.

Benefits of the StraightFlo valve

Fuel savings of 3 – 15%: Gas flows straight through the valve with very low resistance. The StraightFlo valve typically has 40 -80% more effective area than the conventional valves it replaces leading to better efficiency, lower power/fuel consumption and lower engine emissions. For high speed applications (>1000 rpm), ZVI guarantees at least a 10% reduction in valve losses compared to the conventional valves it replaces in an application.
More revenue: Use the power saved by the improved efficiency of the valve to increase throughput of the compressor by reducing clearance or increasing speed. Increase flow by 3-15% in a high speed compressor.
Longer life and higher reliability:   Increase compressor MTBS (Meant Time Between Service) by a factor 5 to >10x. ZVI guarantees a 2x increase compared to the prior valves under the same conditions.
The easiest valve to service, the lowest down-times: No machining of seat or carrier, 5 – 10 minute turn-around time per valve at your site; eliminates machining, special tools, skilled labor. 10 year limited warranty on metal housing.
Lower inventory costs by >80%: Stock standard modules - not complete assembled valves unique to a cylinder and application. Modules are interchangeable for different stages and compressor brands.
Increase service interval: Proven valve design to further improve life of the valve. 5 year limited warranty on valve internals.
Reduce vibration and noise: Because there are no plates/poppets/rings impacting against a valve seat or guard, the vibration and noise levels when using StraightFlo valves is typically 1/10th that of conventional valves.
Green operation: 3 – 18% reduction in fuel consumption, emissions, and carbon footprint. Minimize waste of material. Lowest noise level among valves.
Industry Leading Warranty: Limited warranty of 10 years on valve housing.
Limited 5 year warranty on valve internals for low speed (less than 600 rpm) applications with good quality gas.
