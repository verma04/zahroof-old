Reduce Environmental Impact

  • Higher efficiency valves result in lower fuel consumption for the same work. 10% lower fuel consumption in a typical 1000 rpm high speed pipeline compression application with our standard StraightFlo valves.
  • Reduce fuel consumption and hence emissions with a simple change of valves. Minimize your Carbon footprint.

About Us

Established in 2010, Zahroof Valves Inc. develops, manufactures, and sells reciprocating compressor valves, unloaders and associated products into the global compressor valve market. ZVI is a Delaware Corporation with its HQ in Houston, TX.
The company has developed its disruptive and unique StraightFlo compressor valve using its patented Modular Reed Valve (MRV) technology.
The company's valve provides superior performance and reliability, significant cost savings, and lower emissions compared to conventional reciprocating gas compressor valves.
ZVI's products are applied in a number of applications including Oil & Gas (Upstream, Midstream and Downstream), Chemical plants, Industrial gases, Air, Refrigeration,etc.
